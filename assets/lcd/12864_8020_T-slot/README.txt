# Summary

<b>Update:</b> See [this thing](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2138001) for a variation of this display mount that hangs the LCD display vertically from the extruded aluminum, and allows the Arduino and RAMPS to be mounted directly to the backplate.

This is a display holder for the LCD 12864 Graphic Smart Display Controller for 3D printers. Mine came with [this electronics kit](http://amzn.to/2gHqoLR), however you can purchase this display as a [standalone LCD monitor](http://amzn.to/2gDXsbb). I made it to attach at a 45 degree angle onto 25mm 8020 T-Slot Extruded Aluminum.

<b>Hardware Needed</b>
- 4 M3x25 screws (I only had 3 in my pictures)
- 4 M3 washers and nuts

- 2 each M6 bolts, washers, and T-Nuts for your aluminum extrusion. I used 12mm bolts but your desired length may vary depending on your T-Slot.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: RepRap
Printer: Prusa i2
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2mm
Infill: 20%

Notes: 
Print 'LCD Face' in face down orientation. If your bed adhesion is strong be careful removing it. Recommend pulling it up from the face directly attached to the bed and not by the angled overhang.
